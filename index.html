Introduction

The University Library is one of the most important libraries of Scotland. It offers a variety of services to assist University students and staffs to study and research. In the library, you will get flexible access to the book collections, IT resourses and study spaces. When you are outside the library, you can login to DiscoverED and free to use the online resources. In total, the University Library holds in excess of 3.5 million printed volumes, along with over 1.9 million e-resources.
In the academic year 19/20, there were a total of 259947 items been borrowed. It is down 33.8% compared to the year 18/19. The total number of searches via DiscoverED was 7345318 and an amount of 4920394 online requests had been made.

Our libraries

There are nine university libraries across Edinburgh, each one covers different subject areas.

The main library in George Square is the largest with more than 2,300 study spaces. It opens 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
From 2018.08 to 2019.08, the main library consists 66.2% of total physical borrowing. It was 56.4% ahead of the second one, Law & Europa Library
